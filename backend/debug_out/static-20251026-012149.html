What Sets Tier Three Valuations Apart

Advanced Patient Metrics®

Traditional reports stop at top-line revenue. Our proprietary Advanced Patient Metrics® platform dives deeper, quantifying:

  • Total active, inactive, and hygiene patients
  • Annual attrition and delinquency trends
  • Age-group demographics for targeted growth opportunities
  • Billings per patient versus provincial benchmarks

The result? A 360-degree picture that often places patient goodwill at 3× the value of physical assets—insight competing brokers simply can’t deliver

Learn More

Four Decades of Market Insight

For over 40 years we’ve tracked deal multiples, regional demand shifts, and lender appetites. That historical lens lets us forecast realistic pricing—protecting you from leaving money on the table or over-pricing into buyer silence.

Forecasts You Can Act On

Each report includes a 10-year cash-flow projection and loan-repayment schedule, giving you—and any prospective buyer—clarity on future earnings and financing viability.

Confidential, End-to-End Support

From document collection to final signature, Tier Three “champions” confidentiality at every touchpoint, ensuring staff, patients, and competitors remain unaware until you decide otherwise.

Smooth Transition

Two and half years ago I started the process of finding someone to help evaluate my dental practice for the sale of my share of a cost share relationship to my son and another dentist who had been associates in the practice for about five years. I proceeded to discuss this with a couple potential groups who do practice evaluations. After these discussions, Barb Johns and Tier Three Brokerage was the group I felt very comfortable with. Barb was very personable and explained the process clearly and concisely. On the surface , the sale seemed fairly straight forward and would proceed quickly and smoothly. Over two years later, the sale was finally completed! The different problems we faced along the way are too great to bring into this discussion but suffice it to say that if it hadn’t been for the guidance and tenacity of Barb Johns, the sale would still not be complete or may not have happened at all! Her continuous support, encouragement and insight helped clear the way for a smooth transition and sale. Barb Johns and Tier Three became close allies during this time and my family and I can’t thank them enough for their dedication in helping us navigate the many hurdles we faced. I have no hesitation in recommending Barb Johns and Tier Three to assist anyone with one of the most important transitions in a dentists career.

Consistently looked after my interests over time

My journey with Tier Three started in 2018 when I first met with Neil Presner to discuss my potential transitional plans.

At that time as I was exploring my options, I was not yet ready to proceed with a firm commitment for a full appraisal. What I valued most was that there was no pressure from Neil. He provided pertinent information and patiently walked me through various options, which included continuing with my practice.

Hands down, I unconditionally recommend Tier Three, specifically Neil Presner and Bernie Dolansky for anyone considering a transition plan. Professionalism, Integrity, and Confidential Support from start to finish.

FAQs

How often should I have my practice valued?

Henry Schein Tier Three recommends commissioning a valuation every year. Treat it as a roadmap that lets you correct course well before you plan to sell.

Do I have to be ready to sell in order to request a valuation?

No. The valuation is just as valuable for long-range planning as it is for an immediate transition. Many owners order one several years ahead to benchmark performance and identify growth opportunities, keeping their options open.
